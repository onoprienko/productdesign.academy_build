Introduction

Throughout this course, you will learn the theory of Enterprise Design Thinking from experts and see real examples.

So, what is an experience?

Is it a coffee maker, or is it a person enjoying a cup of coffee?

So, what is an experience?

Think of the last time you had tea, coffee, or hot cocoa. Maybe it was this morning. How did it make you feel? Why did you drink it?

What else were you doing at the time? Chances are your answers to those questions are different than anyone else who drank a hot beverage recently. Those answers represent your experience.
